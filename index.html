How it works
1) User submits a ticket Frontend

Data is validated in the browser, then sent as JSON to an Activepieces webhook.

2) Automation & AI routing Activepieces

Flow categorizes, assigns priority, generates notes, SLA deadline, and auto-reply.

3) Stored & tracked Sheets

Results are stored in Google Sheets for support operations and reporting.
